Who is Elizar

Elizar is a professional human being. He has been breathing air since August 18th, 1989, and and saving lives since February 23rd, 2003 (Continue on Elizar's Influence). Ever since he was a young lad, he felt fuiled by his desire have so much of everything, that he could give to others without worry. He did however get side tracked and poured all of his passion and time into the world renowned real combat simulator, For Honor.
