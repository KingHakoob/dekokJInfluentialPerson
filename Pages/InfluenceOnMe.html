He Saved Me

On February 23rd, 2003, there was a fire, like a really bad fire (It was a very hot fire), at 25.0000° N, 71.0000° W. I was only 2 at the time, and despite what you might think, I couldn't swim. It was just me, my $25 million yacht, my 35 personal assitants, and the sea. The fire started on the top deck, which makes sense because heat rises, but our hero Elizar was completely unaware of what was going on. He was only 13 years old, but he already possessed incredible strength, speed, the ability to fly, and the incredible mind power to read and control minds. For Elizar this was a dream come true, after all his favorite superheros were Professor X and Superman. If you ask me, it was by chance that he heard the minds of my 35 personal assitants. As soon as he realized what was happening he flew as fast as he could to the yacht, took a deep breath, and he blew the fire out. That day not only did he save our lives, he saved me $25 million for another yacht.
